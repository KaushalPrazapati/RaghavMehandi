The Art of Tradition

Welcome to Raghav Mehndi Art, based in the heart of Mithapur, Patna. We believe that applying Mehndi is not just a ritual, but an emotion that connects hearts and traditions. Our intricate designs are woven with love, blessing every bride with deep colors and beautiful stories.

From rich traditional Indian patterns to sleek modern Arabic designs, we customize every detail to match your unique personality and wedding theme.
