Meet Raghav Nayak

Based in the heart of Mithapur, Patna, Raghav Mehndi Art is synonymous with precision, creativity, and luxury. As a pioneer in blending traditional motifs with modern artistry, Raghav ensures every bride feels like royalty.

From intricate bridal networks to bold Arabic strokes, our organic, skin-safe henna guarantees a deep, long-lasting stain. Available for worldwide bookings to bring exquisite art directly to your venue.
